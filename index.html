The 2021 Nobel Laureates

Physics

One half jointly to Syukuro Manabe, Klaus Hasselmann and the other half to Giorgio Parisi - "for groundbreaking contributions to our understanding of complex systems".

Chemistry

Benjamin List and David MacMillan - “for the development of asymmetric organocatalysis”.

Medicine

David Julius and Ardem Patapoutian - “for their discoveries of receptors for temperature and touch”.

About.

Aims

This website aims to provide the world with accurate, detailed data from across the three disciplines of science, give students factually correct descriptions of many scientific concepts, and aid teachers in their teaching of the world around us.

Author's Note
